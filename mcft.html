Masala Cheese French Toast

Give Your Regular French Toast A Masaledar Twist. If you're someone who loves trying out new recipes for breakfast, then this masala cheese French toast is a must-try!


Steps to Make Masala Cheese French Toast

Prepare the Masala

1. In a pan, pour 2 tsp oil.
2. Put half finely chopped onion.
3. Finely chopped chilli as per your need and mix it well.
4. Put 1 finely chopped tomato and mix until mixed properly.
5. Take 1/4th finely choppped Capsicum, 2 tbsp sweet corn, half grated carrot and mix it well uptill 1 minute.
6. Cover and cook it for 5 minutes.
7. Add 1/4th tbsp turmeric powder, half tsp chilli powder, 1 tsp chaat masala, 1/4th tsp crushed pepper, 1/4th tbsp salt and mix it well.
8. Add 2 tbsp tomato sauce and mix it well.
9. Lastly add 2 tbsp finely chopped coriander.
10. And your Masala is READY!


Make the Bread Ready


1. Take a bread and apply butter on both the sides.
2. Toast the bread until it turns golden.
3. Apply one tbsp of green chutney on it.
4. Then apply the prepared masala on to the bread.
5. Put some cheese on it, then cover and simmer it for 2 minutes.



Ready to serve!

Cut it into two pieces, pour some amount of cheese and it is ready to serve!
