Kheer

Kheer is a popular dessert from the Indian sub continent, made primarily with rice,milk and sugar. The word Kheer is derived from the Sanskrit word "Skheer" meaning milk. So milk being the main ingredient in a kheer recipe!



Steps to make Kheer.


1. Wash rice well at least thrice and drain. If you want you may also soak it until the milk is ready and hot.
2. Pour 1/4th cup water. Pour milk and bring it to a boil on a medium flame.
3. When it turns hot or comes to a boil, stir it in the drained rice.
4. Cook on a medium to low heat until the rice is soft, completely cooked and slightly mushy. During this process keep stirring often at the bottom to prevent scorching the milk.





5. Add 4 to 6 tablespoons sugar. You ca add more or less according to your taste.
6. Lower the flame and cook until the sugar dissolves completely and the rice kheer turns thicken.
7. Add cardamom powder, nuts, rose water and saffron.



Garnish rice kheer with more nuts before serving and enjoy the delicious kheer!
